

# About CRMPRO

CRMPRO is a self-hosted CRM system built on top of the Twenty open-source platform, customized and deployed for internal business use. It provides a clean, modern interface for managing companies, contacts, deals, tasks, emails, and calendar events — all in one place.

<br />

# Features

- **Companies & Contacts** — Track your clients, leads, and business relationships
- **Deals & Pipelines** — Manage sales stages with Kanban and list views
- **Tasks & Reminders** — Assign tasks with due dates and get notified
- **Email Integration** — Connect Gmail or Outlook to sync emails directly in the CRM
- **Calendar Sync** — Connect Google Calendar; events created in CRMPRO push to Google Calendar automatically
- **AI Assistant** — Built-in AI chat powered by Anthropic Claude for CRM queries, data lookups, and automation
- **Workflows** — Automate actions with trigger-based workflows
- **CSV Import** — Import records in bulk; imported fields auto-appear as columns
- **Custom Data Model** — Add custom fields and objects to fit your business
- **Roles & Permissions** — Control what each team member can see and do
